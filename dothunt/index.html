WHAT IS
DOT HUNT?

DOT HUNT is a 60-second reflex challenge that will genuinely cook your brain. A glowing dot bounces around the screen — your only job is to click or tap it.

Simple? Sure. Until the dot gets smaller, faster, and your hands start shaking. Miss 3 times and it's over. No checkpoints. No retry tokens. Pure, raw reflex.

Chain hits to build insane combos that multiply your score. Reach Level 5 and the dot is barely visible — a pixel-sized ghost mocking your existence.
