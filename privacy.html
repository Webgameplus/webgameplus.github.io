WebGame Plus is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. We do not sell your personal data to anyone.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate our service effectively. The types of data we may collect include:

Automatically Collected Data:

  • IP address (used for geographic analytics and fraud prevention)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system and device type
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • Date and time of your visit

Data You Provide Voluntarily:

  • Email address (only if you contact us via email)
  • Any message content you send to us

We do not collect your name, home address, payment information, or any sensitive personal data unless explicitly provided by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data for the following purposes only:

  • To deliver and improve our gaming services
  • To analyze website traffic and understand how players interact with our games
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity
  • To respond to messages and support requests
  • To display relevant advertisements through our advertising partners
  • To comply with legal obligations

We never use your data for unsolicited marketing, selling to third parties, or any purpose not listed above.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

WebGame Plus uses browser local storage (not traditional cookies) to save your in-game progress, high scores, and preferences. This data is stored entirely on your own device and is never transmitted to our servers.

What we store locally:

  • Game high scores and personal bests
  • Game progress and unlocked levels
  • User preferences (sound on/off, difficulty settings)

Our advertising partners (Monetag, Adsterra) may use cookies to serve personalized advertisements. These cookies are governed by their respective privacy policies, linked below.

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser's cache and local storage.

4. Third-Party Services & Advertising

We use the following third-party services on our platform:

  • Monetag — advertising network. May collect anonymized usage data to serve relevant ads. View their privacy policy at monetag.com
  • Adsterra — advertising network. May use cookies for ad personalization. View their privacy policy at adsterra.com
  • Google Fonts — used to load web fonts. Google may log font request data. View Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com
  • GitHub Pages — our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect server access logs. View GitHub's privacy policy at github.com/privacy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain automatically collected analytics data for a maximum of 26 months, after which it is deleted or anonymized. Email correspondence is retained for up to 12 months unless you request earlier deletion.

Local storage data (game saves, scores) is retained on your device indefinitely until you choose to clear it.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to Deletion — request that we delete your personal data
  • Right to Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Object — object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Portability — request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at webgameplus.contact@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

WebGame Plus is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete that information.

Our games are designed to be family-friendly and suitable for players of all ages.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the limited personal data we process. Our website is served over HTTPS encryption. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

In the event of a data breach that affects your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of WebGame Plus after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: webgameplus.contact@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: Privacy Request — WebGame Plus
  • Response Time: Within 30 days
