HOW TO PLAY
(If You Can)

01

LOOK AT THE WORD

A large color word appears on screen — RED, BLUE, GREEN, etc. Your brain will desperately want to answer based on what the word says. Resist this instinct or die.

02

IDENTIFY THE COLOR

Ignore what the word says. Focus on what color the letters actually are. If the word "GREEN" appears in red ink, the answer is RED. Simple in theory. Agony in practice.

03

TAP FAST OR LOSE A LIFE

A countdown bar is draining. Tap the correct color button before it hits zero or lose a life instantly. You have 3 lives. The game gets faster. There is no mercy.

04

DON'T TRUST THE BUTTONS

Color buttons are deliberately deceptive. The button labeled "RED" might be colored blue, with orange text. You must match the button's LABEL to the word's actual ink color.

05

SURVIVE 60 SECONDS

The game lasts 60 seconds. Each correct answer scores +1. Each wrong answer −1. Lose all 3 lives before time ends and the game ends early with full humiliation.

06
