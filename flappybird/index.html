How to Play

Simple controls, big fun — anyone can pick it up in seconds!

👆

Tap to Flap

Tap the screen (or press Space!) to make your bird flap its wings and fly upward.

🌿

Dodge the Pipes

Fly through the gaps between green pipes without touching them. One hit and it's game over!

⭐

Score Points

Every pipe you pass = 1 point. Reach score milestones to level up and unlock new titles!
