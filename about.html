Our Story

WebGame Plus was founded with a single mission: to make high-quality arcade gaming completely free and instantly accessible to everyone around the world — no downloads, no sign-ups, no barriers.

We are an independent game development project started by a student developer from Barisal, Bangladesh. What began as a small personal project during a school vacation quickly grew into a growing collection of browser-based games enjoyed by players across the globe.

"I wanted to build something real — games that anyone can play on any device, anywhere in the world, without paying a single penny. That's the dream behind WebGame Plus."

We believe that gaming should be fun, free, and frictionless. Every game on our platform is hand-crafted to run directly in your browser — whether you're on a phone, tablet, or desktop computer.

What We Offer

Our platform hosts a growing library of original browser games across multiple genres:

  • Shooter Games — Bullet Storm Survival with endless waves and boss fights
  • Classic Arcade — Snake, Flappy Bird reimagined with modern neon visuals
  • Puzzle Games — Memory Match, Card Flip, Color Sort, Jigsaw and more
  • Reflex & Skill — Dot Hunt, F1 Reaction, Color Panic challenge
  • Educational — Typing Speed Test, Color Mixer Lab
  • Card Games — UNO and more coming soon

Our Future

We are constantly working on new games and improvements to the platform. Our roadmap includes multiplayer features, global leaderboards, more puzzle and strategy games, and eventually a dedicated mobile app.

If you enjoy WebGame Plus, the best way to support us is to share our games with your friends and family. Every visit helps us keep the lights on and build more great games for you.
