Getting Started

How to Play UNO Online

UNO is easy to pick up, endlessly fun. Here's all you need in 4 steps.

01
🃏

Deal 7 Cards

Both you and the AI start with 7 cards from a full 108-card deck. One card is flipped to start the discard pile.

02
🎯

Match & Play

Play a card matching the top card's color or value. Can't go? Draw exactly one card — then play it or pass.

03
📣

Call UNO!

Hit the UNO button the moment you're down to 1 card. Forget and the AI catches you — 2 penalty cards!

04
🏆

Empty Your Hand

First to play all their cards wins. Score points from your opponent's remaining cards and play another round.

Official Rules

All Official Rules.
Faithfully Implemented.

No house rules. No shortcuts. Every official UNO ruling is in the game exactly as intended.

  • ⊘

    Skip — Lose a Turn

    In a 2-player game, Skip works just like Reverse — the same player goes again immediately.

  • ⇄

    Reverse = Skip in 2-Player

    Official ruling: Reverse causes the current player to take another turn, effectively a self-skip.

  • +2

    Draw Stack Rule

    Stack Draw 2 on Draw 2, or Wild +4 on Wild +4 to pass the penalty to your opponent instead of taking it.

  • 1

    Draw One Per Turn

    Standard rules: draw exactly 1 card when you can't play. You may play the drawn card or pass your turn.
