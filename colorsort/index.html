How to Play
Memory Card Game

1

Flip a Card

Tap or click any face-down card to flip it over and reveal the hidden emoji symbol beneath.

2

Flip a Second Card

Choose another card you think matches the first. Use your memory to remember where you've seen each symbol.

3

Match = Stay Open

If both cards show the same emoji, they stay face-up and glow green. You earn 100 points for every pair found!

4

No Match = Flip Back

If the cards don't match, they flip back face-down after 0.7 seconds. Memorize their positions for later!

5

Match every pair on the board to complete the level. Use fewer moves and less time for a higher score!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is MemoryFlip and how does it work? ▼

MemoryFlip is a free browser-based memory card matching game. A grid of face-down emoji cards is laid out on screen. You flip two cards at a time — if they show the same emoji, they stay face-up (a match!). If they don't match, they flip back after 0.7 seconds. The goal is to find all matching pairs in as few moves and as little time as possible. It's a digital take on the classic "Concentration" card game.

Is MemoryFlip completely free? ▼

Yes — MemoryFlip is 100% free to play with no hidden costs, no premium subscription, no in-app purchases, and no ads cluttering your screen. Simply open the game in any modern web browser and start playing immediately. No account, no email, no signup of any kind.

Can I play MemoryFlip on my phone or tablet? ▼

Absolutely. MemoryFlip is fully optimized for mobile devices. It uses touch-friendly tap controls, features large card targets for easy tapping, runs at a smooth 60fps, and automatically resizes the card grid to fit your screen perfectly — whether you're on a small iPhone, a large Android phone, or an iPad tablet.

How is the score calculated in MemoryFlip? ▼

Your score is calculated using the formula: Score = (Matches × 100) − (Moves × 5) − Time in seconds. You earn 100 points for every successful pair match, but lose 5 points for each total move and 1 point per second elapsed. This rewards players who are both fast and efficient — find pairs quickly with minimal wasted flips to maximize your score.

What are the different game modes? ▼

MemoryFlip has three game modes: Classic mode lets you progress through all 10 levels in order, saving your highest level reached. Daily Challenge generates a unique puzzle tied to the current date — the same puzzle for everyone globally, resetting every midnight. Endless mode randomly picks a difficulty level each time, perfect for casual play without pressure to progress.

Does playing memory games actually improve your memory? ▼

Yes! Multiple cognitive studies have shown that regularly playing memory matching games activates the prefrontal cortex and hippocampus — brain regions responsible for memory formation and recall. Players show improvements in short-term visual memory, attention span, pattern recognition, and processing speed. Playing for even 10–15 minutes daily can produce measurable cognitive benefits over time.

Is MemoryFlip suitable for kids? ▼

Yes! MemoryFlip is designed for all ages. The Level 1 (2×2) and Level 2 (3×2) grids are perfect starting points for young children aged 3 and up. The emoji themes — especially Animals and Fruits — are engaging and age-appropriate. The game contains no violence, no ads, and no external links, making it completely safe for children to play unsupervised.
