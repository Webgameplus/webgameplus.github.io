Why Contact Us?

Here are the most common reasons people reach out to us:

  • Report a bug or broken game on a specific browser/device
  • Suggest a new game you'd like to see on the platform
  • Ask about business partnerships or advertising opportunities
  • Report inappropriate content or copyright concerns
  • Ask general questions about the website or games
  • Submit feedback about your gaming experience
  • Request removal of personal data (GDPR/privacy requests)
Business & Advertising

Interested in advertising on WebGame Plus or discussing a business partnership? We welcome collaboration opportunities with brands that align with our gaming community.

For business inquiries, please email us with the subject line "Business Inquiry — WebGame Plus" and include details about your proposal. We will respond within 2 business days.

Before You Contact Us

Please check the following before sending a message — it might save you time:

  • Try refreshing the page if a game is not loading
  • Make sure your browser is up to date (we support Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Disable browser extensions that might block game scripts
  • Check your internet connection for smooth gameplay
