Frequently Asked Questions

Is Puzzle Party completely free?+

Yes — 100% free with no hidden costs, no in-app purchases, no premium levels, and absolutely zero ads. Just open it and play instantly.

What age group is it designed for?+

Ages 3 to 12. The Easy 3×3 levels are perfect for toddlers, while Expert 5×5 levels challenge older kids and even adults!

Does it work on tablets and phones?+

Absolutely! Full touch support for iOS and Android. Also works on all desktop browsers — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Do I need an account or download?+

No account and no download! Just click Play Free Now and start instantly. Progress is automatically saved locally on your device.

What are the real jigsaw shapes?+

Unlike games using simple squares, Puzzle Party generates real interlocking pieces using bezier-curve tabs (bumps) and slots (holes) — exactly like a physical jigsaw puzzle!

Is it safe for children?+

Completely safe! No ads, no external links, no social features, no chat, no user-generated content. Just a pure, focused puzzle game with age-appropriate nature scenes only.

What are the educational benefits?+

Develops spatial reasoning, pattern recognition, problem-solving, fine motor coordination, concentration, and visual memory. Kids also learn about nature and geography through the puzzle scenes.
