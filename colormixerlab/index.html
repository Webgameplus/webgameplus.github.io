How to Play

Four simple steps to color mastery. No experience needed!

Step 01
🎯

See the Target

A mystery color appears on screen. Your mission: figure out which paints to mix to recreate it perfectly!

Step 02
🖌️

Pick Your Paints

Tap any paint blob to add it to your mix. Add more of one color to make it stronger in your blend!

Step 03
✅

Check Your Mix

Hit Check! to see your accuracy score. The closer your mix, the more stars and points you earn!

Step 04
🏆

Level Up!

Crush all 12 levels and become the ultimate Color Master. Use your 3 hints wisely across the journey!
