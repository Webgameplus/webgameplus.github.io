WHAT IS
F1 REFLEX?

F1 REFLEX simulates the most nerve-wracking moment in motorsport — the starting grid. Five red lights ignite one by one. When they all go dark, a dot flashes somewhere on screen. You have milliseconds to hit it.

Average F1 drivers react in ~200ms. Most people take 400ms+. Every round the window shrinks, the dot gets smaller, and your composure collapses. 3 lives. 10 rounds. No second chances.
